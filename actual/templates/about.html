Welcome to SWAP book !
Ever wanted a better way to find cheap textbooks for your new classes? Tired of spamming open lists trying in vain to sell that book you should have just rented? Despair no longer -- SWAP book is here!

Created by Harvard sophomores with similar frustrations, SWAP book empowers you to post used textbooks as a seller, search for textbooks as a buyer, and coordinate sales right here on Harvard's campus.

Whether to make up some of that cash you sunk in an overpriced textbook (@Greg) or to avoid such a tragedy all together, SWAP book has your back!
